# GTTP User Manual Summary

Welcome to the GTTP App! This quick manual outlines the primary flows for users navigating the platform.

## 1. Logging In
1. Open the application.
2. Enter your registered email address and your password.
3. Tap **Login**.
4. You will receive an OTP. Enter the 6-digit code into the boxes provided and tap **Verify**.

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard
Once logged in, the **Dashboard** acts as your central hub. 
- You can find quick-access grids pointing to the **School Network**, **Gallery**, **Data Export**, and **Student Progress**.
- Your overall activity statistics are visible at the top.

## 3. Viewing Notices
1. Tap the **Notices** icon (bell symbol) at the bottom of the screen.
2. If there are new announcements, you'll see an orange numbered badge.
3. Tap on any notice in the list to read its full description and details.

## 4. Managing Courses
1. Tap the **Courses** icon (book symbol) at the bottom.
2. You will see a list of courses you are enrolled in or that are open.
3. Tap **View Course** on a specific course to see its modules.
4. From the Course Detail screen, you can watch video materials, take interactive quizzes, and upload assignment files.

## 5. Editing Profile
1. Tap the **Profile** icon (person symbol) at the bottom.
2. Review your current demographic and school details.
3. Tap the **Edit** button to update your name, photo, or phone number. Save changes to update your profile globally.
